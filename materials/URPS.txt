These are the non-functional requirements for ShuffleBoard.

The app shall run on Android.
The user will install through their app store.
A beginning user should be able to create their schedule easily.
Information such as FAQs and documentation will be provided and accessible to users.
The user should be able to see their schedule on start-up.
The system should remember the user's entered schedule even after possible crashes.
The system should remember the other users the user has shared their schedule with.
ShuffleBoard should start-up in at least three seconds.
The app will be maintained by the Raging Narwhals at least until the semester is over.
The system should know the difference between static and dynamic events.
The user must have a schedule with dynamic events before being able to shuffle, as shuffling beforehand will not change thhe schedule.
